About Me

Hi friends! My name is Aaron-Josh Vietvu, but I also go by AJ for short. I'm a digital media art major from San Jose State University and I love and appreciate all kinds of art. This website will showcase some of my very own pieces. My interest in art started at a very young age. When I was in preschool, I was really into drawing. By the time I was in elementary school, I started drawing anime characters. In middle school, I started to draw things from my own mind. By high school, I really just drew anything I wanted. I even started painting. I also got into photography and other forms new media art.
